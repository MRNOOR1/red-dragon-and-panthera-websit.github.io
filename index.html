What defines a gym

Google definition of gym is “A gym is a club, building, or large room, usually containing special equipment, where people go to do physical exercise and get fit.” But that’s not what our trainers see.

Our Process

During the past 10 years, we have trained many of the world’s best athletes, and have been known for our special way of teaching and educating people based on their character. There are no two people who have been trained the same at our gym as we believe no one is the same.

Our Approach

Many of our students have experience and overcome some of the hardest situation faced by modern humans including poverty and addition, yet our team have successfully helped many people to overcome and change, with the right method and mindset. Just like with their techniques, we teach our students the right way of approaching problems in real life.

Our Goal

Our trainers believe the greatest goal is the goal which can be reached. Therefore our goal is to create a better and healthier world and teach people the right way of solving problems.

Meet the Team

  • Grand Master Relson Gracie

    Relson used to be called “Campeão” (meaning: Champion), a name earned amongst the Rio de Janeiro surfing community who saw with much respect his jiu jitsu activities. Often his friends would call his house looking for the “Campeão” and his mother would get angry on the phone yelling back “In this house everyone is a champion! Which one do you want?”

  • Grand Master Vladimir Vladimirovich Putin

    Putin has described judo as "my favorite sport", and he continues to practice it. In 2004 he co-authored a book about judo, published in Russian as Judo with Vladimir Putin and in English as Judo: History, Theory, Practice.

  • Liam Harrison

    As befits his nickname of “the hitman”, Harrison has over 40 wins ending in KO with powerhouse punches and deadly low kicks. He is a 3-time Leeds Sports Federation Sportsman of the Year between 2004 to 2008 as well as recipient of the Muay Siam Magazine Foreign Fighter of the Year in 2007.
